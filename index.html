FAQ

Common Queries

Do I need to book in advance for Bridal Makeups?
Yes, we recommend booking at least 3-4 weeks in advance for wedding makeovers. This allows us to lock the dates with our lead makeup artists and arrange a custom pre-bridal grooming outline.
What products do you use for hair coloring?
We use exclusively original Schwarzkopf Professional and L'Oréal Professionnel colors. Our styling equipment includes high-end Dyson dryers to protect hair follicles from heat damage.
Are your Academy certificates globally recognized?
Yes, all certifications issued by Gliter Shine Academy align with industry standard curriculums. Graduates receive formal course completion certificates accepted by salon chains across the country.
